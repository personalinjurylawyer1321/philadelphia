Consultation FAQ

How much does the consultation cost?
The consultation is completely free with no hidden costs or obligations. We only get paid if we successfully recover compensation for you.
How long does a consultation take?
Most consultations take 30-60 minutes, depending on the complexity of your case. We'll take as much time as needed to fully understand your situation.
What should I bring to the consultation?
Bring any documents related to your accident: police reports, medical records, insurance correspondence, photos, and witness information if available.
Can I have a consultation over the phone?
Yes, we offer phone consultations for your convenience. We can also meet at your home or hospital if you're unable to come to our office.
What if I decide not to hire you after the consultation?
There's no obligation to hire us. The consultation is free regardless of your decision. We'll still provide you with valuable legal guidance.
How quickly can I get a consultation?
We can often schedule consultations the same day you call. For emergency situations, we're available 24/7 including weekends and holidays.
